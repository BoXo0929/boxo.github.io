CampingGo is a mobile app designed for camping enthusiasts. What sets it apart from other shopping platforms is its integrated community feature, allowing users to explore real-life experiences shared by others. This helps users make more informed decisions about which products are best suited for their own camping adventures.

challenge

Limited Product Range: Many camping apps or websites may not offer a comprehensive selection of gear and supplies for outdoor activities in Canada, such as products suited for harsh winters or unique terrains. This can frustrate users who need specialized equipment.

High Prices and Limited Discounts: Camping gear is often expensive, and users may be frustrated by a lack of sales, discounts, or rewards programs tailored to frequent campers.

Lack of Community Features: Many users want to share their experiences or see recommendations from fellow campers. Without social or community-driven features, users miss out on advice and peer validation.

Lack of Real-time Reviews: Trustworthy reviews are crucial for outdoor equipment, but many apps may not offer up-to-date, user-generated reviews, leaving shoppers uncertain about product quality.
